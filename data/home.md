# How I Build Products

## Building products starts with understanding people.

I believe great products are rarely the result of a brilliant idea—they're the result of deeply understanding a problem.

Every product I've worked on started with conversations, observations, and continuous iteration. Rather than assuming what users need, I prefer validating ideas early, shipping small, measuring outcomes, and improving quickly.

My engineering background helps me collaborate effectively with developers, understand technical constraints, and make realistic product decisions—but I believe the best product decisions always begin with customer insight.

Whether I'm working on an AI healthcare platform, an internal productivity tool, or a marketplace, my goal remains the same:

Build products that solve meaningful problems and create measurable business impact.
